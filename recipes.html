Winter Blues Blend

Enjoy this recipe on those cool frosty mornings to warm yourself up before the day starts, or to just relax at home by the fireplace.

  • 1oz. Bonicet
  • 1oz. Ecinacea
  • 2oz. Peppermint

Echinacea works as an immune system builder, boneset is great for congestion, aches and fever, and peppermint aids with any stomach complaints from the sinuses, a great overall “feel-good”.
